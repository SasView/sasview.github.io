It is with great pleasure that we announce the opening of registration for the 4th [SasView Contributor Camp](https://github.com/SasView/sasview/wiki/ContributorCampXV) 
(formerly known as Code Camp XV) to be held at Charles University in Prague, Nov 3-9 2026.

This annual community event brings together members of the SAS community to work on improving 
the SasView tools, tutorials, and documentation that we all use. The final scope of the work is 
determined by the interests of those who attend but known points of interest/focus this time 
include the General Scattering Calculator (making it more user friendly while better supporting 
bioSAS needs), the sasmodels package, and improving support for hard condensed matter and 
diffraction workflows.

Anyone interested in contributing to the SasView project (writing tutorials, documentation, coding, 
verifying the math/science, or undertaking sys admin tasks, etc) is welcome, even encouraged, to 
join. See the link below for the latest planning. This is an opportunity to come together to work 
on our common issues.

The camp planning page can be found at: https://github.com/SasView/sasview/wiki/ContributorCampXV

Registration is at: https://workshops.ill.fr/event/586/registrations/395/

We look forward to seeing many of you there.
