Acknowledgments/Contacts

if you found this sofware useful to your work please dont forget to acknowledge its use in your publications as suggested below and reference this website: SasView, http://www.sasview.org/. Please also consider letting us know by sending us the reference to your work. This will help us to ensure the long term support and development of the software.

This work benefitted from SasView software,originally developed by the DANSE project under NSF award DMR-0520547

  • To report bugs or for general help using the software please email: help@sasview.org
  • For general help with fitting and modeling etc try our emailing the users forum: users@sasview.org
  • For code questions and discussions contact the developers at: developers@sasview.org
  • For general information about Small Angle Scattering we recommend the SAS portal developed by the canSAS group.
