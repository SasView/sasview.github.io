The principle socio-economic output of the SasView project is the scientific understanding that flows from its application to SAS data measured on a tremendous variety of samples across many different fields of study. This output is typically recorded in publications ('papers') in scientific journals. The list below details those publications where SasView was used in the data analysis phase of the investigation.

The list is compiled by searching the databases of journal publishers. Whilst every effort has been taken to ensure the accuracy of the list, the SasView Project cannot be held responsible for third-party errors that may have crept in.

The list is ordered ALPHABETICALLY by the name of the journal, then CHRONOLOGICALLY by the date of publication.
